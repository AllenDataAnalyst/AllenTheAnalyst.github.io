Financial Statistics Dashboard

Dashboard Contents & Analysis:

    The first dashboard analyzes income sources:

    • An interactive and dynamic web chart provides a detailed analysis of all sources of income and the percentage of each source, highlighting the source with the highest value.
    • A creative doughnut chart shows the achieved percentage from the target.
    • Dashboard title and description.
    • Total Income amount and the target.
    • A line chart for income by months.
    • Total counts and percentage of Income Sources items.
    • Average of monthly income.
    • A bar chart showing the Monthly operating profits and the total amount.
    • Analysis for two types of Marketing strategies.
    • All these values are controlled and changed based on the year selection of the slicer.

    The second dashboard analyzes total profits by countries:

    • Creative design for a dynamic map chart.
    • Analysis of profit value from each country, with highlights for the most profitable country.
    • Details of various types of taxes.
    • The percentage of profits achieved using a doughnut chart with circular edges.
    • Fully controlled by yearly slicer.

    The third dashboard is for sale process status:

    • A diagram that explains the stages that all sales processes go through, showing the number of customers at each stage and highlighting the most frequently used stages.
    • Doughnut chart showing total sales and the percentage of sales achieved from the target.
    • Sales by points of sales.
    • Chart for the total refunded orders percentage.
    • Bar chart for orders by points of sales.

    The fourth dashboard is for a project's workflow report,

  • with several main KPIs in a beautiful design updatable directly from the data-table.

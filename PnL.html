PROFIT and LOSS & BALANCE SHEET

Unveiling Insights and Driving Success with Comprehensive Financial Data Analysis and Visualization

Task:

  • Visually present the company's financial story from 2016 to 2019, showcasing profitability (P&L) and financial health (Balance Sheet).

Action:

Summary

  • Concise overview of revenue, expenses, net income, assets, liabilities, and equity.
  • KPI cards with current values, comparisons to previous periods, and trend indicators.

P&L Insights

  • Trend analysis of revenue, cost of goods sold (COGS), and gross profit.
  • Visual breakdown of operating expenses leading to net income.
  • Calculation and display of profit margins (gross, operating, net).

Balance Sheet Insights

  • Comparison of total assets and liabilities with year-over-year changes.
  • Detailed view of asset and liability categories using pie charts or treemaps.
  • Key ratios: debt ratio, current ratio, working capital, debt-to-equity ratio, with trend indicators.

Result:

  • Provide stakeholders with a comprehensive understanding of the company's financial performance and health.
  • Enable data-driven decision-making across departments.
  • Facilitate trend monitoring for early identification of issues and opportunities.
  • Enhance transparency and communication around financial performance.
